Why it feels good

An arcade prototype with real momentum.

Pressure from every direction

Fight air threats with laser bolts, crush ground targets with bomb payloads, and keep your fuel reserve from turning every mistake into a crisis.

Stage-driven atmosphere

Distance-based segments keep the run shifting, with changing skies, terrain profiles, enemies, and visual mood as you push farther out.

Play instantly, replay immediately

Built for a keyboard-first flow with quick restarts, fullscreen toggle, remappable controls, and a tight arcade feedback loop.

Moodboard energy

The kind of sci-fi atmosphere Starkiller is chasing.

Gigantic horizons

Warm suns, deep space, hard-surface silhouettes, and a horizon line that feels like a promise.

Neon dream color

Cyan, orange, and magenta drive the same retro-futurist contrast the game leans into.

Readable sci-fi drama

Big shapes, dramatic lighting, and space that feels dangerous even when it looks beautiful.
